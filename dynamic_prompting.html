Dynamic Prompting (Adaptive AI)

🔄 Understanding Dynamic Prompting

🎯 What is Dynamic Prompting?

Dynamic prompting creates AI that adapts its behavior based on initial findings, patient context, and clinical scenarios. Unlike static prompts, it modifies its approach in real-time for more personalized analysis.

🏥 Clinical Relevance

Mirrors how doctors adapt their examination based on initial findings. A pediatric case requires different considerations than an elderly patient, and emergency presentations need rapid assessment protocols.

🔬 Adaptive Intelligence

The AI performs an initial assessment, then intelligently chooses the most appropriate follow-up analysis based on complexity, patient demographics, and clinical context.

⚡ Progressive Analysis

Analysis deepens progressively: initial screening → complexity assessment → adaptive follow-up → refined diagnosis. Each step informs the next for optimal clinical relevance.

🔍 Understanding Chain of Thought Prompting

🎯 What is Chain of Thought?

Chain of Thought prompting makes AI show its step-by-step reasoning process before reaching a conclusion. Instead of jumping to an answer, the AI demonstrates how it thinks through the problem systematically.

🏥 Medical Relevance

Mirrors how doctors present cases: systematic examination → pattern recognition → differential diagnosis → final conclusion. This transparency builds trust and enables learning from AI reasoning.

🔬 Transparent Process

Every diagnostic decision is auditable and explainable. You can see exactly why the AI reached its conclusion, making it safer and more educational than "black box" AI.

🎓 Educational Value

Demonstrates proper medical reasoning patterns. Students and professionals can learn from AI's systematic approach to diagnostic thinking and clinical reasoning.

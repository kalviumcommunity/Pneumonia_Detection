Zero-shot prompting is a technique where we ask the AI model to perform a task without providing any examples or training data for that specific task. The model relies entirely on its pre-trained knowledge to understand and execute the task.


Key Characteristics:

  • ‚úÖ No Examples: No training examples or demonstrations provided
  • ‚úÖ Clear Instructions: Detailed task description and requirements
  • ‚úÖ Pre-trained Knowledge: Relies on model's existing medical knowledge
  • ‚úÖ Immediate Application: Can be applied to new tasks without fine-tuning

ANALYSIS REQUIREMENTS:
1. Systematically examine both lung fields
2. Look for abnormal opacity, consolidation, or infiltrates  
3. Assess for signs of inflammation or infection
4. Consider image quality and positioning
5. Evaluate pleural spaces and heart borders

RESPONSE FORMAT:

CONFIDENCE: [Number from 0-100]%
REASONING: [Detailed explanation]
KEY_FINDINGS: [Specific radiological observations]
RECOMMENDATIONS: [Next steps]
